1. Overview

Muon ("we," "us," or "our") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our mobile application, Muon — Sleep & Focus Sounds (the "App").

Privacy promise: Muon is a fully offline app. We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal data to our servers. There are no servers.

2. Data Collection

Muon does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. The App operates entirely offline and does not require an internet connection for its core functionality.

Specifically, we do NOT collect:

  • Personal identification information (name, email, phone number)
  • Device identifiers or advertising identifiers (IDFA / IDFV)
  • Location data (GPS, Wi-Fi, IP-derived)
  • Usage analytics or behavioral data
  • Health, medical, or sleep tracking data
  • Audio recordings from your device's microphone
  • Photos, contacts, calendar, or any other user content

This corresponds to the Apple App Store privacy label: "Data Not Collected".

3. Analytics & Tracking

We do not use any third-party analytics, tracking, advertising, or crash reporting SDKs. No Firebase, no Sentry, no Mixpanel, no Facebook SDK. No data is sent to any third-party service. The App does not attempt to identify you across other apps or websites.

Apple's privacy practices governing these transactions: apple.com/legal/privacy

5. Data Storage

Your sound preferences, saved mixes, sleep timer settings, and entitlement status are stored only locally on your device using iOS's standard app sandbox (UserDefaults / SQLite). They are not synced to iCloud unless you opt in via iOS settings, and are never uploaded to any Weaveon server. If you delete the App, this data will be permanently deleted. Your purchase entitlement can be restored on any device signed into the same Apple ID via the "Restore Purchase" option.

6. Children's Privacy

Muon is suitable for all ages (rated 4+) and contains no user-generated content features, social features, or interactive communication tools. While the App includes a "Baby Soothing" sound category, it does not collect any information from or about children.

In compliance with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA), we do not collect personal information from children under 13. Since we do not collect personal information from anyone, this requirement is automatically satisfied.

7. Your Rights

7.1 GDPR (European Economic Area / UK / Switzerland)

If you are located in the EEA, UK, or Switzerland, you have the rights under the GDPR to access, rectify, erase, restrict processing of, port, or object to processing of your personal data, and to withdraw consent. Since we do not collect any personal data, these rights are automatically satisfied.

Legal basis: We do not process personal data. Local on-device storage is necessary for the App's core functionality (Article 6(1)(b) GDPR).

Data Protection Officer: Weaveon does not have a designated DPO as we do not engage in large-scale data processing. Direct all inquiries to flashbody@foxmail.com.

7.2 CCPA (California, USA)

California residents have the rights under CCPA to know, delete, opt-out, and not be discriminated against. We do not sell, share, or disclose any personal data. Contact: flashbody@foxmail.com

7.3 Other Jurisdictions

Users in other jurisdictions may have additional privacy rights under local laws. We respect all applicable privacy laws and will respond to legitimate requests within the required timeframes.

8. Data Retention

We do not retain any personal data on our servers (we have no servers). All user preferences are stored locally on your device and are deleted when you delete the App.

9. Third-Party Services

The App uses Apple's StoreKit framework for in-app purchases, governed by Apple's privacy policy. The App also uses Apple's AVFoundation for local audio playback. No other third-party services or SDKs are integrated.

Audio content includes Creative Commons licensed field recordings sourced from Freesound.org (CC0 and CC-BY), bundled with the App at compile time. Freesound.org is not contacted at runtime.

10. Security

Since the App does not collect or transmit personal data, the risk of data breach is inherently minimal. Locally stored preferences are protected by iOS's standard sandboxing and Data Protection (file encryption tied to your device passcode).

11. International Users

The App is available globally. Since no data is collected or transmitted, there are no cross-border data transfer concerns.

12. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The "Last updated" date at the top of this page will reflect any changes. Material changes will be highlighted within the App. Continued use of the App after any changes constitutes acceptance of the updated Privacy Policy.
