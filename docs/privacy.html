Muon — Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2026

Data Collection

Muon does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. The app operates entirely offline and does not require an internet connection.

Analytics

We do not use any third-party analytics, tracking, or advertising SDKs.

Purchases

In-app purchases are processed entirely through Apple's App Store. We do not have access to your payment information.

Data Storage

Your sound preferences and saved mixes are stored locally on your device only. They are not synced to any server.

Children's Privacy

Muon does not knowingly collect information from children. The app contains no user-generated content features.
