Frequently Asked Questions

Q: Is Muon free?

Muon is free to download with a selection of sounds. To unlock all 18 sounds and pro features, a one-time purchase of $3.99 is available. No subscriptions, ever.

Q: How do I restore my purchase on a new device?

Open Muon → Settings → tap "Restore Purchase". Make sure you're signed into the same Apple ID used for the original purchase. Restoration is free and unlimited across all your devices.

Q: Sound stops when I lock the screen — how do I fix it?

Muon supports background playback. If sound stops:

  • Open Settings → Focus and ensure Muon isn't restricted
  • Make sure your iPhone isn't on Silent Mode (the side switch)
  • Check that Low Power Mode isn't aggressively suspending background audio

Q: How does the Sleep Timer work?

Tap the timer icon, choose a duration (15 / 30 / 45 / 60 / 90 minutes), and Muon will gently fade out the sound and stop playback when time runs out. Fade-out can be toggled in settings.

Q: Can I mix multiple sounds?

Yes! Tap the mixer icon and add up to 4 sounds simultaneously. Adjust each volume independently with the sliders. You can save your favorite mixes for one-tap recall.

Q: Is the Baby Soothing Mode safe for infants?

Yes. Sounds in Baby Soothing Mode are designed at gentle volumes inspired by clinical research. Always keep the device at a safe distance from the baby (at least 6 feet / 2 meters) and at a moderate volume. Consult your pediatrician for personalized advice.

Q: Does Muon work offline?

Yes — 100% offline after the first download. Muon does not collect any data, does not require an account, and never sends any information to our servers.

Q: How can I request a refund?

Refunds are handled by Apple. Visit reportaproblem.apple.com, sign in with your Apple ID, and select Muon to request a refund. If Apple denies your request and you believe there's a legitimate issue, email us at flashbody@foxmail.com and we'll do our best to help.

Q: I have a feature suggestion — how do I share it?

We love hearing from users! Email us at flashbody@foxmail.com with the subject "Feature Suggestion" — we read every message.

Q: Will Muon support Apple Watch / CarPlay / Mac?

macOS support (via iPad app) is already available. Apple Watch and CarPlay support are on our roadmap — let us know if you'd like to see them prioritized!
